About this template

The first amazing aspect about this template is that, anyone can download this template for free! It follows bootstrap layout that your eyes have been used to when you were born to surf the internet. For example, the navigation bar above is what most people expects on tap, while it automatically shrinks to an icon in the upper right on smaller devices. Various sections can be easily accessed via this navigation bar. There is no waiting time to jump from one section to another, because this is a responsive one-page design. Just above here, the big heading would be visible upon landing, followed by a 1-2 sentence(s) that immediately tells the visitors who you are.


It is very common in sites so that users can decide if they want to read more about it by clicking a button that expands the text. In this way, you can save a lot of space, while keeping all sections of your site in good proportion and rhythm. Most people like to scroll your page, especially on a mobile device, when they first see your page. So you definitely do not want to be overloaded with too much information, including pictures (which take time to load). Keep it simple so that your site visitor can scroll from one section to another in a pace that is comfortable. A good test is to take a page and give yourself 10 seconds to scroll past it while seeing if your can easily capture the major headings and points.

Philosophy

So many musicians have wasted hundreds of dollars on a site that ends up to be hard to navigate and visually too complex. When this happens, your site traffic and stickiness suffers. So you might ask, how much freedom do you have if you are interested in a making a site like this? Our recommedation is that you would probably keep the same sequence and flow as far as the sections. You have absolute freedom when it comes to replacing the text and assigning the number of items, especially in the to-be-followed sections below. A different color scheme is definitely possible. In this template, we use the "Facebook" color scheme. You might not want to change the font too drastically, because there are fonts that are known to be standard, easy to read, and compatible with different operating systems. Please keep in mind that you do not necessary want to dump your entire CV and every single performance on this page. You want to capture someone's attention by not overloading the number of choices they have.
